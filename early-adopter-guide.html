Early Adopter Guide

Get the app running in under 5 minutes

EARLY ACCESS PROGRAM

Thank you for joining! You are among the first to try How Are You?! — an app that uses AI to keep elderly parents safe. No wearables, no cameras, no daily check-in calls. Just their Android phone.

Follow the steps below. Everything takes about 5 minutes.

1
Join the Early Adopter Group

Open the link below and tap "Join group" (or "Ask to join"). This registers your Google account so you can download the app.

Join the Google Group groups.google.com/g/howareu-early-adopters
Use the same Google account that is on your Android phone. If you have more than one Google account, make sure you pick the one your phone uses.
2
Accept the Testing Invitation

Open this link. You will see a page from Google Play:

Open Testing Invitation play.google.com/apps/testing/com.howareyou

Tap the green "Become a tester" button:

Tap "Become a tester"

After you accept, the page changes. You will see "You are a tester" and a link to download the app:

You are now a tester — tap "download it on Google Play"

Done! You are now registered. Continue to Step 3 to download the app.
3
Download the App

Choose whichever is easier for you:

📱

On Your Phone

Open this link directly on your Android phone and tap "Install"

Open Google Play
🌐

On the Web

Visit our website from any device and tap "Download"

Visit howareu.app
If Google Play says "App not available" — go back to Step 1 and check that you joined the group and accepted the invitation with the same Google account that is on your phone.
4
Redeem Your Promo Code

As an early adopter, you get the app completely free. Your personal promo code will be shared in the Google Group.

Your Promo Code
XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
Check the Google Group for your personal code

Here is how to redeem it:

  • Open the app and go to the subscription screen
  • Tap "Subscribe" — this will open Google Play checkout
  • Look at the list of payment methods
  • Scroll to the bottom of the list and tap "Redeem code"
  • Type or paste your promo code and tap "Redeem"
That's it! The app is now fully unlocked at no cost to you. No credit card needed.
Each code works only once and is tied to one Google account. Please do not share your code with others.
5
Set Up the App

Open the app and follow the on-screen steps. The setup takes about 3 minutes:

  • Allow permissions — the app needs location and activity permissions to monitor safety
  • Turn off battery optimization — this lets the app run in the background 24/7 (the app will guide you through it)
  • Add a family email — this is who gets alerted if something unusual is detected
After setup: The app starts a 7-day learning phase where it quietly observes daily routines. After that, monitoring begins automatically. There is nothing else you need to do — it's designed to be install-and-forget.
